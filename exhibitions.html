Exhibitions

Cosmology

Explore the wonders of our cosmos. Our fantastic exhibition, ‘The Sky Above Us’, explores the night sky and what we can see and know about the universe around us.

Evolution

Where do we come from and how did we get here? Discover the origins of life on this planet and how the species we know today have evolved our time.

Biology and Medicine

From micro-organisms to the human body, major breakthroughs in biology are offering us unique insights into the great wonders of the tiny world.

Robotics and AI

The robotics and artificial intelligence are the science of the future. Would you like to say hello and shake the hand of Rob the Robot?

Ecology

In our ecology section of the museum, we unpack the dangers we currently face. We focus on ways we can all contribute to a brighter future.
